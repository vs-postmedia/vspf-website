About the Festival

The Vancouver Street Photo Festival is an international celebration of street photography coming to Vancouver, BC in June 2027. Rooted in the raw, unposed energy of life as it unfolds, the festival brings together photographers, artists, and audiences from around the world.


At its core is an open call competition — open to photographers at every level — whose winners and finalists will be showcased in a curated public exhibition. Surrounding the exhibition: a full programme of speakers, panels, workshops, and events exploring the art, ethics, and culture of photographing in public space.


More details — venue, dates, submission guidelines, speakers, and ticket information — will be announced in the months ahead. Sign up below to be the first to know.
